BelaMed Trading Plc is an authorized reseller of the Integrated Hospital Information Management System (IHIMS), one of Dart's flagship products.

Integrated Hospital Management System Modules

An Integrated Hospital Management System (IHMS) typically includes a variety of modules designed to streamline operations and improve patient care.

Patient Management

  • Patient Registration: Capturing patient demographics, medical history, and insurance information.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Managing appointments, cancellations, and rescheduling.
  • Admission and Discharge: Handling admission procedures, bed allocation, and discharge processes.
  • Electronic Medical Records (EMR): Storing and managing patient medical records, including vital signs, diagnoses, and treatment plans.

Clinical Services

  • Laboratory Information System (LIS): Managing laboratory tests, results, and reporting.
  • Radiology Information System (RIS): Handling radiology exams, images, and reporting.
  • Pharmacy Management: Managing medication inventory, prescriptions, and dispensing.
  • Nursing Documentation: Tracking patient care activities, medication administration, and vital signs.

Financial Management

  • Billing and Insurance: Generating bills, managing insurance claims, and tracking payments.
  • Revenue Cycle Management: Streamlining the billing process to improve cash flow.
  • Accounts Receivable: Managing outstanding payments and collections.
  • Inventory Management: Tracking medical supplies, equipment, and inventory levels.

Administrative Functions

  • Human Resources: Managing employee information, payroll, and benefits.
  • Facility Management: Managing building maintenance, equipment upkeep, and asset tracking.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Generating reports on key performance indicators (KPIs) and analyzing data for decision-making.
  • Security and Access Control: Ensuring data security and managing user access privileges.

Value Proposition for Integrated Hospital Management System

Hospital Business Owners

  • Increased Efficiency: Streamline operations, reduce administrative burdens, and improve overall efficiency.
  • Enhanced Financial Performance: Improve revenue cycle management, reduce costs, and increase profitability.
  • Improved Decision Making: Access real-time data and analytics to make informed decisions.
  • Enhanced Patient Satisfaction: Provide better patient experiences and improve patient loyalty.
  • Compliance Adherence: Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards.

Patients

  • Enhanced Patient Care: Access to timely and accurate medical records improves patient outcomes.
  • Reduced Wait Times: Efficient appointment scheduling and reduced waiting times enhance patient satisfaction.
  • Better Communication: Improved communication with healthcare providers and staff for a more personalized experience.
  • Seamless Billing: Transparent billing processes and insurance claim management simplify financial interactions.

Health Development Partners

  • Collaborative Efforts: Enable partnerships with health organizations and stakeholders.
  • Data Sharing: Share data for research, public health efforts, and collaborative projects.
  • Program Impact: Improve the effectiveness of public health programs through comprehensive data analytics.
  • Resource Optimization: Utilize shared resources and services to maximize impact.

Government

  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensure healthcare facilities meet all government regulations and standards.
  • Public Health Surveillance: Monitor and track health data for public health initiatives and responses.
  • Policy Implementation: Streamline the implementation of new healthcare policies and programs.
  • National Health Goals: Contribute to the achievement of national healthcare objectives through data-driven strategies.
